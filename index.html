Mahatma Gandi

BIOGRAPHY

Mahatma Gandhi was born on October 2, 1869, in Porbandar, India. He led India's struggle for independence from British colonial rule using nonviolent resistance. Gandhi is known for his philosophy of truth (Satya) and nonviolence (Ahimsa). His iconic actions include the Salt March and Civil Disobedience Movement.
